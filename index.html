Facts About Messi

  • He is an Argentine soccer player who wears the number 10 shirt for Barcelona.
  • He has scored over 700 career goals, mostly with his left foot, and has more than 360 assists.
  • He has won more than 30 trophies, including 10 La Liga titles and seven Ballon d'Or awards.
  • He is the all-time leading scorer for both Argentina and Barcelona, and holds the record for the most goals in a calendar year (91 in 2012).
  • He is 5′ 7″ tall and has three sons with his wife, Antonella Roccuzzo.
